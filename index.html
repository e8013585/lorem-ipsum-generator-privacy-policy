Overview

Lorem Ipsum Generator ("the Extension") is a browser extension that generates placeholder text directly within your browser. Your privacy is fully respected: no personal information is collected, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history, tabs, or website content
  • Authentication credentials or cookies
  • Geolocation data
  • Device fingerprints or system information
  • Analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking data

Local Storage

The Extension uses the chrome.storage.local API to save your preferences between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

Data Purpose Location
Selected mode Remember your last chosen generation mode (paragraphs, words, bytes, or lists) Local device only
Quantity value Remember the last used amount Local device only

This data can be cleared at any time by removing the Extension from your browser. No external server ever has access to it.

Permissions

The Extension requests the following minimal permissions:

Permission Reason
storage To save your preferred generation mode and quantity locally on your device

The Extension also uses the Clipboard API (navigator.clipboard) to copy generated text to your clipboard when you explicitly click the "Copy" button. No clipboard data is read or transmitted—only written to when you request it.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, load content from, or communicate with any third-party services, APIs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external servers. All text generation happens entirely offline within the browser.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other demographic.
